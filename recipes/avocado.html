Weird and Wonderful Avocado Chocolate Peanut Butter Pudding

ingredients

  • ripe avocados, peeled and sliced
  • ¼ cup + generous pinch of cocoa powder (more to taste)
  • ¼ cup peanut butter
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup agave nectar (I used the amber variety)
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice

instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor until velvety smooth.
  2. Refrigerate for 12-24 hours. Serve topped with banana slices and halved peanut butter cups to really wow ‘em!

HAVE FUN!!
